# Code Of Conduct

TaskFence is a security-focused open-source project. We expect direct,
technical, respectful collaboration.

## Standards

Expected behavior:

- Focus criticism on code, behavior, evidence, and tradeoffs.
- Assume security reports and safety concerns deserve careful handling.
- Keep discussions free of harassment, personal attacks, and discriminatory
  language.
- Do not pressure reporters or contributors to disclose private vulnerabilities,
  secrets, customer data, or identifying details in public channels.
- Respect maintainer decisions about scope, readiness claims, and security
  boundaries.

Unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, threats, insults, or sustained disruption.
- Publicly posting exploit details, credentials, private URLs, or sensitive
  logs.
- Misrepresenting unsupported TaskFence behavior as production supported.
- Repeatedly bypassing review, security, or governance processes after being
  asked to stop.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or delete comments, close issues, block accounts, or take
other repository-level action when behavior harms the project or its users.

Security vulnerabilities should be reported through [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md),
not through public conduct reports or public issues.
